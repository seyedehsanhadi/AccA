1.0.44

Fixes charging pulsing on and off near the limit (for example 77% with a 75% limit: charge ~20s, stop, repeat). The bundled ACC daemon (fix4) no longer briefly re-enables charging while it is pausing on devices whose charging switch the firmware keeps re-arming, and it now picks a switch once per session instead of re-testing it every loop. The limit is also enforced fail-safe: a missing or unreadable pause/resume level is treated as "pause now / do not resume".
