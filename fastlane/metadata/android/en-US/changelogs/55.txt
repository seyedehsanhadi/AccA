1.0.51

Fixes the upper charge limit being overshot on Pixel/Tensor. The only working switch there (charge_stop_level) is a charge limit, not an on/off — the bundled daemon now sets it to your target level so the firmware holds the battery flat at the cap (no overshoot, true idle) instead of sawtoothing past it. The switch scanner now prefers flat-hold switches. Bundles ACC v2025.5.18-dev-fix7. If you previously locked a switch, tap "Scan & fix charging switch" once more after updating.
